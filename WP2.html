Our Working Packages

WP2: Education

Empowering Future Researchers

WP2 aims to develop educational resources, conduct summer schools, and provide training in biogeodynamics research methodologies.

  • About
  • Objectives
  • Impact

WP2 ensures knowledge dissemination through structured learning modules and interactive workshops.

Developing comprehensive training materials and organizing summer schools across Europe.

Fostering a new generation of biogeodynamics experts by providing high-quality educational resources.

Addressing climate change, biodiversity, and sustainability.

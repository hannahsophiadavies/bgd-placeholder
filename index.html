Biogeodynamics

COST action CA23150 - pan-EUROpean BIoGeodynamics network (EUROBIG)

WG 1 Networking

Coordinating conferences and workshops. Supporting early career researchers to build research networks and attend and participate in conferences. Also concerned with organising a dedicated Biogeodynamical conference.

WG 2 Education

Organising educational events related to the COST action focused particularly on training early career researchers. These events include: Summer schools, online classes, and seminars.

WG3 Tools

Highlighting and detailing many of the most popular and useful tools for conducting cross disciplinary Biogeodynamic research. This can include but is not limited to models, data repositories, numerical code, data processing tools and training documents.

WG4 Outreach

Increasing public awareness of Biogeodynamics in various ways inside and outside of academia. The outreach team is essentially the news outlet for the action communicating the newest results and implications.

WG5 Future

Enabling and coordinating generation of funds for continued Biogeodynamical research once this COST action has ended. The discipline of Biogeodynamics is still young and continued collaboration and funding is required to fully explore the new avenue of research.

WG6 Society

Exploring in particular, the implications of Biogedodynamic research in the context of building a sustainable furture for humanity. Biogeodynamic research covers societal issues including but not limited to climate change, biodiversity, sustainability, exploitation of resources in a sustainable way.

WG7 Council

A small group of members of the action consisting of leadership from each of the other WGs who will guide the action and collect research being done in Biogeodynamics.

About Us

Our main goal is to bridge geodynamics, climate, and biosphere research

EUROBIG is a collaborative initiative dedicated to unraveling the complex interactions between Earth's geodynamics, climate, and biosphere. Through interdisciplinary research and cutting-edge models, we aim to bridge scientific gaps and shape the future of biogeodynamics.

EUROBIG (Pan-European Biogeodynamics Network) is a COST Action initiative dedicated to advancing research on the interactions between geodynamics, climate, and the biosphere. Our network unites experts from diverse fields to develop interdisciplinary frameworks for understanding Earth's evolution and its future dynamics.

To foster collaboration among geoscientists, climate researchers, and biologists, creating a unified platform for biogeodynamics research. EUROBIG aims to bridge knowledge gaps and develop predictive models for Earth’s changing environment.

Developing courses, summer schools, and educational materials.
